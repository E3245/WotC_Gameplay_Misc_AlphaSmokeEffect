[h1][b]DESCRIPTION[/b][/h1]
[h1]-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.[/h1]

Alpha Smoke was inspired by an idea from XCom: UFO Defense, where smoke breaks Line of Sight from other units (Aliens, Humans, etc.). While it is impossible to even change Line of Sight rules or dynamically change an obstable to block LOS, I feel like this is a good compromise between those elements.

If you have issues with this mod or spotted a bug, please report it in the [url=link1]Bug Report Thread[/url]. If you want to provide feedback, whether its good or bad, then post in the [url=link2]General Feedback Thread[/url].

If you are thinking of downvoting this mod, please provide feedback first, so I know what I should improve on/did wrong.

[h1]-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.[/h1]
[h1][b]HOW IT WORKS[/b][/h1]
[h1]-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.[/h1]

Smoke Grenades are neutral items. It grants +20 Defense Buff but a -20 Aim malus while in smoke. In addition, anyone in Alpha Smoke has immunity to Overwatch.

Some caveats:
[list]
	[*]You must be in smoke initially to get the overwatch immunity.
	[*]You can go through smoke without ever triggering overwatch.
	[*]Overwatch will trigger on the very exact tile that does not have smoke.
	[*]You cannot be outside of the tile that doesn't have smoke and cross through smoke to get the immunity if the enemy unit has full view of the cloud of smoke.
	[*][b]Overwatch, Suppression, Return Fire, Close Quarters Specialist, or any Overwatch derivatives are disabled while in smoke[/b], at least basegame wise. Custom mod abilities are not supported out of the box.
[/list]
